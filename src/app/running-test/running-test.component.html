WARM UP

  • Run-walk 10-15 minutes easy. Once you feel fully warmed up, do the strides.
  • 5 x 20 second strides, followed by 40 seconds easy jog between.
    (*a stride is a quick burst of speed, where you accelerate for the first part, maintain almost max speed for the middle and gradually decelerate for the remainder. You should concentrate on good form and fast feet)

THE ASSESSMENT

  • Immediately following the warmup, you will run for 20 minutes at the fastest pace you can sustain for this duration. This should be about 80-90% of your max effort. It will be hard. Stay strong! It is better to find the rhythm in the first few minutes and make sure the effort is sustainable than it is to start off too hard and fade. Hit the lap timer when you start and end this segment.

COOL DOWN

  • Walk EASY for 10 minutes
